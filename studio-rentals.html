We refer to our in house events as “Encore Posh Parties”. Posh parties are small events with big style! If you are planning a celebration for 60 people or less, let us host it for you. If you’re not celebrating but just need a place to meet we can accommodate that too!

Our celebration pricing includes our exclusive décor. Since we are designers, unfortunately no outside decorations are permitted. We also have a preferred caterer whose food is AMAZING, however you are permitted to bring your own food. Please see the pricing and rental information below.
