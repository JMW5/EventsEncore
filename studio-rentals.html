STUDIO RENTALS

Did you know that you can rent our studio? We refer to our in house events as “Encore Posh Parties”. Posh parties are small events with big style! If you are planning a celebration for 50-60 people or less, let us host it for you. If you’re not celebrating but just need a place to meet we can accommodate that too!

Our celebration pricing includes our exclusive décor. Since we are designers, unfortunately no outside décor is permitted. We also have a preferred caterer whose food is AMAZING, however you are permitted to bring your own food. Please see the pricing and rental information below.



Encore Posh Parties are great for intimate:

  • Bridal Showers
  • Baby Showers
  • Teas
  • Birthday Parties
  • Holiday Parties
  • Anniversaries
  • Rehearsal Dinners

See our Posh Party designs on our Portfolio page

If you book your complete wedding with us, please take advantage of 15% off our studio rental! Perfect for your bridal shower, rehearsal dinner or engagement party.
